About Me

I commenced my South India trip in 2012 with a Point and Shoot camera. Like for most of the travellers and photographers, travelling and photography made a great combination for me. In mid-2014, when I was travelling in UK shooting landscapes, I decided to take Photography as a career. In early 2015, I upgraded my gear to DSLR, got professionally trained, attended multiple workshops, worked on various projects.

Today, I am a professional photographer and shoot a wide range of subjects and topics - landscapes, portraits, fashion, new-born, kids, food, and products. I am based out of Hyderabad and travel around for assignments. Along with the above range of topics, I do take assignments for birthdays, engagements, weddings, model portfolio, fashion shows, and corporate events - collaborating and partnering with fellow photographers and studios.
